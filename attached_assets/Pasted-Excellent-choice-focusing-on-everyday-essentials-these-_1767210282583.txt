### Data Sources (All Public & Reliable)
All data will be sourced from trusted government or industry bodies to ensure accuracy and credibility:
- Milk, Bread, Eggs, Ground Beef, Gasoline: BLS National Average Price series (monthly, 1980–present)
- Electricity: EIA/U.S. Energy Information Administration (monthly residential average, 1970s–present)
- New Car: Bureau of Economic Analysis / NADA average transaction price (annual/monthly compiled)
- Big Mac: The Economist Big Mac Index (semi-annual, 1986–present)
- First-Class Stamp: USPS historical rate table (discrete steps since 1919, very clean data)
- Movie Ticket: NATO/The Numbers annual average (1970s–present)
